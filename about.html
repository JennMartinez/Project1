About us

A little bit about who we are, and why we made this place.

Jenn

Michael

Sergio


Jenn, Michael, and Sergio are friends and classmates in a web development program. When it came time to begin work on a group project, Jenn and Michael came to the realization that no truly safe place exists for those who identify as gay and lesbian to find love online. Together with Sergio, they set out to create such a place. This is the result.


It is their hope that, using this website, you can connect with others who desire a safe place to connect and, hopefully, find love.
